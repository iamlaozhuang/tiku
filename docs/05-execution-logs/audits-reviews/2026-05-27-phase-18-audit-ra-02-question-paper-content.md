# Phase 18 Audit RA-02 Question Paper And Content

**Date:** 2026-05-27

**Task:** `phase-18-audit-ra-02-question-paper-content`

## Scope

Audit RA-02-01 through RA-02-11 against `docs/01-requirements/stories/epic-02-question-paper.md`, `docs/01-requirements/modules/02-question-paper.md`, architecture contracts, static implementation, unit coverage, and local browser/e2e evidence.

This report records facts and findings only. No business bug fixes are made in this audit task.

## Phase 17 Prerequisite Context

- Local DB, dev server, and Playwright e2e are generally usable.
- `student` and `super_admin` can be audited with real local login evidence.
- Persistent local `content_admin` login accounts are missing. Role-specific browser evidence for content authoring is therefore `partial` or `blocked` unless explicitly synthetic.
- Real provider, staging/prod/cloud/deploy, env/secret, dependency, and destructive data gates remain blocked.
