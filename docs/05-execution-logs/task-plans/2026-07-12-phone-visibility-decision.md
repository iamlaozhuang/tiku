## Decision To Materialize

1. Phone remains the immutable learner/employee login identifier. Display is separate from identity and authorization.
2. Student self-service, organization-admin, and employee surfaces return/display a masked phone only. They cannot reveal or copy a full phone.
3. `ops_admin` and `super_admin` user/employee management lists return/display masked phones by default and permit exact phone search.
4. Only a qualified `ops_admin` or `super_admin` may request one full phone for one user through a dedicated, service-authorized, non-cacheable action. Reveal and explicit copy are audited without recording the full phone in audit metadata.
5. Exports, ordinary audit views, error feedback, list/detail defaults, and import results do not return a full phone. Import input remains necessary to create/bind an employee, but later output remains masked.
6. Prelaunch AI paper results lacking an assembled persisted snapshot remain readable only. They are not backfilled, reconstructed from current sources, or regenerated by a Provider. Test-data refresh is a separate, later destructive-data decision.
